Our Vision...

Students are the future of the world and learning is what they do.

We encourage them to learn more by providing a platform to enhance their skills, innovate and share their ideas.

With Cazime, a student will be able to showcase his work, skills, ideas, achievements and goals to the world.

He will be able to easily find, interact with, share with, discuss with and develop with other people having similar interests and ideas.

Our Mission

Interconnect students of departments, colleges and universities in a sophisticated way to let them interact with other students.

Students will chat, discuss, share their work, study materials, thoughts and ideas with the targeted audience which can be a specific person, branch, college, university, place or perceiving a similar interest.

Using ‘Cazime’ as a companion to find the right people, to help the students grow in their career and to help them grab every opportunity to learn.

About Us

Cazime is a start-up founded by senior undergraduate of IIT BHU, Hariom Bairwa(CEO),

which is aimed at bringing and connecting all the students of IITs and NITs on one platform in order to innovate and share their knowledge and views with each other

where you can share your projects, presentations, models, internship and interview experiences;

where you can find a mentor to seek some guidance or help you in a start-up or a project you are working on;

where you can ask a query and others will answer it; where you can find people having similar interests to interact/work with;

where you can improve the quality of your social contacts for a better all-round development of your academic and professional career.
